# Project Interpretation

This project is designed as a **market regime intelligence and crisis risk research platform**.

The objective is to understand how markets evolve across hidden states and how downside risk accelerates.

---

## Regime Meaning

### Calm
Low volatility and stable market expectations.

Characteristics:
- low dispersion
- narrow distribution
- stable beliefs

---

## Stress
Volatility expansion and uncertainty clustering.

Characteristics:
- elevated variance
- asymmetric downside
- belief instability

---

## Crisis
Extreme tail-risk and rapid state escalation.

Characteristics:
- downside shock dominance
- crisis probability spikes
- high belief velocity

---

## Practical Applications

This framework can be extended for:

- portfolio risk management
- macro regime analysis
- crisis early warning systems
- downside stress testing
- volatility forecasting
- risk analytics research

---

## Research Value

The project combines:
- empirical statistics
- probabilistic state inference
- dynamical systems intuition
- 3D risk visualization

This makes it suitable for quantitative research and risk modeling roles.
